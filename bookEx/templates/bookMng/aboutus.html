    About BookExchange

    A community-driven platform connecting readers, sharing stories, and giving books a second life.

    🎯

    Our Purpose

    BookExchange was built by students at Cal State LA (CS3337) with a simple mission: make books more accessible and reduce waste by helping readers trade, discover, and connect over literature.

    Every book has a story — not just the one printed on its pages. Our platform gives books a second life by connecting people who have finished reading with those who are eager to begin. Whether you're a casual reader or a passionate bibliophile, BookExchange is your digital community bookshelf.

    ♻️ Reduce book waste
    🤝 Build community
    📖 Promote reading
    ⚡

    Features & Services

    📤

    Post a Book

    List a book you'd like to exchange. Add a title, author, description, and cover photo so other readers know what's available.

    🔍

    Browse Books

    Explore all available books on the platform. Find your next great read posted by members of the community.

    📋

    Book Details

    View full details of any listing, including description, condition, and who posted it, before deciding to request an exchange.

    🗂️

    My Books

    Keep track of all the books you've posted in one convenient place. Manage or remove your listings at any time.

    ⭐

    Rate Books

    Share your thoughts by rating books you've received through the platform. Help the community make informed choices.

    🔐

    Secure Accounts

    Register and log in securely. Your account keeps your listings and activity safe and easily accessible.
